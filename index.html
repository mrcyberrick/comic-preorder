How it works

Three steps a month. That's it.

01

Import the month

Drop in the Lunar and PRH catalogs. Every title is priced, dated, and ready to browse — no retyping.

02

Customers reserve themselves

They browse the month, reserve titles and variant covers, and subscribe to the series they never want to miss.

03

Bag it on release day

Every release day you get a per-customer bagging list — who gets what, already sorted and ready to check off.
